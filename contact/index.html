About Me

I am a graphic designer living in North Carolina, and working with clients all over the United States

In my spare time, I love to get away from the computer and go on long walks, try new recipes, and spend quality time on the couch, making crafts and (I should be honest) watching TV.

My work has been featured on Design*Sponge, Oh So Beautiful Paper, and in Brides Magazine.

You can follow along with my personal adventures over on my blog, or connect with me through social media!
